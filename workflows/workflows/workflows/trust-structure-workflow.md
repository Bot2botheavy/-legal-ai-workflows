# Trust & Financial Structuring Workflow

## Step 1: Entity Setup
- Establish trust structure
- Define roles (trustee, beneficiary)

## Step 2: Account Structuring
- Open trust account
- Separate personal vs trust funds

## Step 3: Asset Allocation
- Assign assets to structure
- Maintain documentation

## Step 4: Transaction Flow
- Define inflow/outflow rules
- Maintain records for all activity

## Step 5: Compliance & Oversight
- Track reporting obligations
- Maintain internal controls
