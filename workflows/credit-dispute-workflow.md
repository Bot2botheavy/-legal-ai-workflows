# Credit Dispute Workflow (FCRA)

## Step 1: Identify Errors
- Incorrect balances
- Invalid dates
- Duplicate accounts
- Charge-offs with inconsistent status

## Step 2: Gather Evidence
- Credit report
- Supporting documents
- Prior dispute results

## Step 3: Send Dispute
- Send written dispute to CRA
- Include identification + evidence

## Step 4: Track Timeline
- 30-day reinvestigation period

## Step 5: Escalate
- Direct dispute to furnisher
- CFPB complaint if needed
