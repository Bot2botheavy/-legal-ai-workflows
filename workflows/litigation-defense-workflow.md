# Litigation Defense Workflow (Consumer Debt)

## Step 1: Initial Case Review
- Review complaint
- Identify causes of action
- Check jurisdiction and service

## Step 2: Procedural Defense
- Motion to dismiss (if applicable)
- Challenge sufficiency of pleading

## Step 3: Discovery Strategy
- Request production (chain of title, agreements)
- Interrogatories
- Requests for admission

## Step 4: Evidence Positioning
- Force business records foundation
- Identify gaps in documentation

## Step 5: Pre-Hearing Strategy
- Prepare argument outline
- Identify procedural leverage points

## Step 6: Hearing Execution
- Focus on burden of proof
- Attack admissibility of records
