# Document Analysis Workflow

## Step 1: Intake
- Identify document type (contract, statement, report)

## Step 2: Structural Breakdown
- Parties involved
- Dates and timelines
- Key obligations

## Step 3: Identify Inconsistencies
- Date mismatches
- Invalid entries
- Missing information

## Step 4: Legal Relevance
- What claims does this support?
- What defenses does this enable?

## Step 5: Extract Action Points
- Dispute items
- Evidence gaps
- Follow-up requests
